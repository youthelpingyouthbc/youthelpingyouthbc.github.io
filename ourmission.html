Testimonials

" Being the president and marketing head of YHY BC has allowed me to broaden my horizons while working alongside a diverse range of passionate colleagues. I have thoroughly enjoyed fine tuning my graphic design abilities, leadership skills and communication. It is rewarding to watch as the hard work and dreams of our passionate team come to the fruition! "

- Trinity Ma, President

" As a content creator in the marketing department, YHY BC has provided a platform for me to outlet my love for creativity and design. YHY BC has strengthened my skills in marketing while building bonds with passionate peers on my team. "

- Goldspring Ha, Marketing Member

" Despite being the first non-profit organisation I volunteer at, I felt very comfortable when I first joined Youth Helping Youth nearly two years ago! Youth Helping Youth has allowed me to grow my leadership, social, and collaboration skills immensely whilst also providing me with an opportunity to learn specific skills such as web design effectively. "

- Ramika De Silva, Head of Marketing

" Being in YHY for the past two years has not only helped me improve my public speaking and leadership skills, but it has also led me to so many new opportunities, helped me make new friends, and taught me a lot about the importance of a great team culture. Honestly wouldn't be where I am today if I didn't join YHY. "

- Donald Lee, Former Head of IT

" There are always new event ideas floating around and I believe that the welcoming environment here at YHY encourages these new ideas to come to life. I’ve been able to make many new and valuable connections and I hope that in the future there will be more created! "

- Emily Qin, Head of HR

" When I started at YHYBC as a Finance Member, I learned a lot of technical and leadership skills such as grant-writing and communication. These experiences have tremendously helped me as the Events manager; from organizing educational events like the 2021 Post-Secondary Panel and YOUTHHACKS 2022 to collaborating with Marketing and Human Resources on event promotion and hiring intakes, I have met so amazing people and developed an interest in program/project/business development and management! I recommend anyone who is passionate about making an impact in their local community to join YHYBC because you not only learn transferable skills, but also become part of a family! "

- Cathy Ha, Events Manager

" Youth Helping Youth has helped me in many ways. Being part of YHY the past 2 years I have learned to apply skills such as organization, accountability, time management, and creativity to other aspects of my life such as my education. I think the biggest way YHY has helped me is inspiring me to work with a brilliant minded group of people and support one another. "

- Parveen Brar, Head of Admin
