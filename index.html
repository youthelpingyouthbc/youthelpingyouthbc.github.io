YOUTHHACKS 2022

YouthHacks 2022 is a hackathon hosted by YHY BC from March 18-20, 2022 that will bring together youth for a weekend full of learning! A variety of events to help you develop your knowledge in STEAM and various workshops by respected speakers will give you some real life insights into the world of STEAM! Decided by our judges, prizes will be given to the best projects on the final day. Best of all, attending YouthHacks 2022 is completely free for all attendees! Beginners are welcome and encouraged to join!

Attendees will network with diverse like-minded youth across the world, learn about coding and programming, and be inspired to explore on their own. Our goal is to make YouthHacks an annual event where we can connect students to the possibilities of technology, while providing students with networking opportunities and resources to explore STEAM. We strive to present Youth with opportunities within the STEAM field that they otherwise would not receive due to the lack of accessibility in schools.

Youth Helping Youth is a registered non profit organization that has successfully hosted numerous events since its beginnings in 2019. Before COVID-19, we were able to host various in-person events including university panels, workshops, fundraisers, and fairs. Currently, in the midst of the pandemic, we have hosted online university panels, game nights for our Discord members, and other youth oriented events.

WHAT IS A HACKATHON?
A hackathon is typically a 24-48 hour coding event where participants can form teams of a maximum of 4 to create a product within our theme of “Helping youth” and present it to a panel of judges. There will be prizes for winning teams, and activities, workshops, and various other events that we will be organizing throughout the Hackathon. Hackathons are a great way for participants to meet new people and develop new skills!

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions you may have about YouthHacks 2022 or hackathons in general!

Do I need a team to participate?

You can sign up individually or with a team. If you don’t have a team yet, you can build one on our Discord server!

How much does it cost?

Nothing! Our Hackathon is entirely free to attend!

Will YouthHacks 2022 be online or in-person?

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, YouthHacks 2022 will be 100% virtual!

Can I work on my project before the event?

No, we prohibit participants from bringing and continuing a project they have already worked on, and your final project should be new and only built during the week of the YouthHacks 2022.

What if I’ve never coded before?

No worries! This hackathon is completely beginner-friendly, and coding workshops will be hosted throughout our event!

Who can attend?

All high school students or university students under the age of 24. Beginners are welcome and encouraged to apply!
